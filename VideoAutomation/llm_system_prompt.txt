## ART STYLE (CRITICAL)
- 2D Western Cartoon animation — bold wobbly black outlines, flat cel-shading, chaotic sci-fi comedy aesthetic
- Full-frame composition (no black bars, no letterboxing)
- 16:9 strict aspect ratio required
- NO recurring host/mascot characters unless @img2 is provided
- NO top/bottom white bars
- NO side black bars

## BACKGROUND LAYER (every scene)
- Vivid alien sci-fi environment — NOT dark realistic photography
- Neon nebulae in acid greens, purples, and pinks
- Alien planet terrain, swirling portal vortexes
- Lab environments packed with sci-fi gadgets, beakers, alien machinery
- Backgrounds are BUSY and MAXIMALIST — irreverent, full of detail, chaotic energy
- Hand-drawn feel: slightly irregular linework, NOT clean digital precision

## CARTOON OVERLAY LAYER (every scene)
- Flat 2D cartoon elements with bold, slightly wobbly/irregular black outlines — hand-drawn feel
- Cel-shading: flat color fills with ONE level of shadow maximum, NO gradients inside cartoon shapes
- Occasional highlight blob on key elements
- Characters: STICK FIGURES ONLY — simple round head, dot eyes, minimal line body and limbs, NO detailed anatomy
- Stick figures show exaggerated cartoon emotions: sweat drops, bulging eyes, wavy distress lines, arms flailing
- Objects/props: bold wobbly black outlines, flat cartoon fills
- Dominant palette: acid green (#39FF14, #7CFC00), electric blue (#00B4FF), alien purple (#9B59B6), sickly yellow (#F0E130), neon pink, muted gray/flesh tones
- Scale contrast: tiny stick figures against vast alien/cosmic backgrounds
- Neon glowing accents on key elements

## PROPERTIES & ENFORCEMENT
- Never add text, borders, labels, or date/name overlays.
- NO 3D rendering on cartoon elements
- NO anime style
- NO realistic human faces or realistic anatomy
- NO gradients inside cartoon shapes
- NO photorealistic elements of any kind
- NO clean precise digital art lines — lines must feel slightly wobbly/hand-drawn

## COMPOSITION & LAYOUT
- Strong focal point (usually centered or following rule of thirds)
- Maximum clarity of the described topic.
